Who We Are

Real-World Solutions.

Helvetia Construction Management GmbH is a Switzerland-based consultancy specializing in marine, civil, remote locations and development projects worldwide.

We provide practical expertise for developers, EPC contractors, and investors seeking broad project leadership or consultancy with flexibility.

We focus on short to medium-term key project leadership roles, project recovery assistance, and technical advisory services that allow clients to stabilize, accelerate, or de-risk their projects and ensure they are structured and supported from their onset.
